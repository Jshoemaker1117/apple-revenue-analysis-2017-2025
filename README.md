# Apple Product Revenue Analysis (2017–2025)

This project analyzes Apple’s product-level revenue composition using official SEC Form 10-K filings from Fiscal Year 2017 through Fiscal Year 2025.

Objective

To evaluate:

iPhone revenue concentration

Services expansion

Revenue mix transformation

Hardware vs recurring monetization dynamics

Long-term compounding differentials

Key Findings

iPhone revenue peaked in volatility during 2021 but normalized thereafter.

Services revenue compounded at ~16% annually, nearly 3x faster than Hardware revenue (~6% CAGR).

Services contribution increased from ~14% of total revenue in 2017 to over 26% in 2025.

Apple’s revenue structure is gradually shifting from hardware concentration toward ecosystem-driven monetization.
